Most language learning apps focus on gamification at the expense of actual learning. They teach you how to click buttons, but not how to speak. Fluly changes this paradigm.

## The Problem with Traditional Apps
Traditional apps rely heavily on multiple-choice questions. This promotes passive recognition rather than active recall. You might recognize the word for "apple" when you see it, but can you produce it in a conversation?

## The Fluly Approach
Fluly emphasizes **active recall** and **production**. From the very first lesson, you are prompted to construct sentences and speak them aloud. 

## Context is King
Instead of learning isolated words, Fluly teaches vocabulary in the context of useful, real-world sentences. This helps you grasp grammar intuitively, just like a native speaker.
